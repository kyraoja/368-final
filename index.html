About Me

My name is Kyra Oja and I am a Visual Communication Design student at Eastern Washington University. I currently work at the EWU Extended Campus as a graphic designer, and earlier this year was a graphic design intern for the Spokane Shock Arena Football Team. I work with digital and print mediums, creating logos and identity branding, and enjoy creating invitations and announcements. I am fluent with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and know how to use Adobe Premiere and Dreamweaver. I am currently building my skills in HTML5 and CSS3.
